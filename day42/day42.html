Crêpes' recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 0.5l milk
  • 4-5 eggs
  • 250-300g flour
  • 50ml hot water
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1-2tbsp sugar
  • 10-15ml oil

Fillings, that can be used:

  • Ground meat
  • Cottage cheese
  • Apples

Instructions

  1. Pour third part of milk to the bowl.
  2. Break all eggs to the same bowl.
  3. Add milk and mix. The dough should feel like joghurt. You might need more flour than mentioned in ingredients
  4. Add the rest of the milk. The dough should be almost like water.
  5. Add salt and sugar.
  6. Add hot water right before frying as well as oil.
  7. Fry crêpes on hot pan. If they stick, add one more egg.
